I build infrastructure for intelligence, markets, and expensive decisions.

Most of my work sits below the visible product: model-serving paths, caching, latency budgets, reliability standards, market maps, and developer platforms that make complex systems easier to operate.

I have led machine-learning platform work at Apple, built production systems at Amazon, and worked on market-structure research at the Bank of Canada. The through-line is simple: find the constraint, make it measurable, and build the smallest durable system around it.
